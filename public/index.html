About

A way to connect to Entertainment

  • Our Humble Beginning

    We started being a student in Front End Web Development bootcamp online. It was a great opportunity to learn and grow as a developer. We have to learn new technologies and figure out ways to solve problems on our own. We also gained experience in working with other students, which is a great way to start a new project with the team.

  • The Project is Born

    During our time in bootcamp, our team were discussing how web was changing, we wanted to find a way to use the web to help people and make it easy for them to locate a particular place or establishment. We have decided to start a project called "Cinema finder App", which would help people to easily find the cinema nearest to their current location. We are going to launch the project by the end of March 2023 and looking forward to its continuous growth as we keep improving and updating this project.

  • Transition and Further Development

    One of our team's top priorities for the project is ensuring a smooth and successful transition from development to implementation. We are aiming to add more features to the project and we are confident that our team have the skills and experience necessary for the future development of this project.

  • Our Future

    We are very excited to continue working on this project and see it through to its completion. We believe that this project has the potential to improve the lives of many people and we are committed to make that happen.
